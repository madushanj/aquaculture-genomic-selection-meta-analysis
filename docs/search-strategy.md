## Database-Specific Search Strings

### PubMed/MEDLINE

```
((("genomic selection"[Title/Abstract] OR "genome-wide prediction"[Title/Abstract] OR 
"genomic prediction"[Title/Abstract] OR "genomic breeding"[Title/Abstract] OR 
GEBV[Title/Abstract] OR "genomic estimated breeding value"[Title/Abstract] OR 
"genomic breeding value"[Title/Abstract] OR GBLUP[Title/Abstract] OR 
"genomic BLUP"[Title/Abstract] OR RRBLUP[Title/Abstract] OR 
"Bayesian genomic"[Title/Abstract] OR BayesA[Title/Abstract] OR 
BayesB[Title/Abstract] OR BayesC[Title/Abstract] OR RKHS[Title/Abstract] OR 
"SNP-based prediction"[Title/Abstract]) 
AND 
(aquaculture[Title/Abstract] OR aquatic[Title/Abstract] OR salmon[Title/Abstract] OR 
trout[Title/Abstract] OR tilapia[Title/Abstract] OR carp[Title/Abstract] OR 
catfish[Title/Abstract] OR shrimp[Title/Abstract] OR prawn[Title/Abstract] OR 
oyster[Title/Abstract] OR mussel[Title/Abstract] OR clam[Title/Abstract] OR 
abalone[Title/Abstract] OR seabass[Title/Abstract] OR "sea bass"[Title/Abstract] OR 
seabream[Title/Abstract] OR "sea bream"[Title/Abstract] OR barramundi[Title/Abstract] OR 
"fish farming"[Title/Abstract])
AND
(prediction[Title/Abstract] OR accuracy[Title/Abstract] OR breeding[Title/Abstract] OR 
"breeding value"[Title/Abstract] OR "predictive accuracy"[Title/Abstract] OR 
correlation[Title/Abstract]))
AND
(2009:2025[pdat]))
```

**Filters**:
- Date: 2009 onwards
- Language: English
- Article types: Journal Article, Review, Conference Paper

### Scopus

"genomic breeding" OR GEBV OR "genomic estimated breeding value*" OR 
"genomic breeding value*" OR GBLUP OR "genomic BLUP" OR RRBLUP OR 
"Bayesian genomic" OR BayesA OR BayesB OR BayesC OR RKHS OR 
"SNP-based prediction") 
AND 
(aquaculture OR aquatic OR salmon OR trout OR tilapia OR carp OR catfish OR 
shrimp OR prawn OR oyster OR mussel OR clam OR abalone OR seabass OR 
"sea bass" OR seabream OR "sea bream" OR barramundi OR "fish farming")

## Search Management

### Citation Management Software

- **Tool**: Zotero (or EndNote)
- **Library**: Shared among review team
- **Organization**: Tags by database and search date

### Deduplication Strategy

1. **Automated deduplication**: Using citation manager
2. **Manual review**: Check for potential duplicates
3. **Criteria**: Same authors, year, title
4. **Documentation**: Track number removed at each stage

### Record Keeping

For each database search, record:
- Database name and platform
- Search date
- Search string used
- Number of results
- Any filters or limits applied
- Searcher initials

**Template**:
```
Database: PubMed
Date: 2025-12-09
Searcher: [Initials]
Search String: [Full string]
Results: [Number]
Notes: [Any relevant information]
```

## Sensitivity Analysis

1. Expand date range pre-2009
2. Include additional languages (with translation)
3. Broaden species terms
4. Contact experts for unpublished work

- Number of records identified from each source
